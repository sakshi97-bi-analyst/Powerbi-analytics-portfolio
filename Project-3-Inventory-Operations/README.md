Business Problem
Operations teams lacked visibility into inventory movement, stock health, and warehouse distribution efficiency, leading to potential overstocking and capital lock-in.

Analyst Objective
Develop an operational dashboard to monitor inventory performance and identify inefficiencies across SKUs and warehouses.

Key Metrics
- Inventory Turnover
- Average Closing Stock
- Inventory Value
- SKU Movement Classification
- Units Sold vs Units Received

Tools Used
Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Business Intelligence

Key Insights
- Identified slow-moving SKUs contributing to excess inventory
- Highlighted warehouse-level inventory imbalance
- Enabled monitoring of stock accumulation risks

Business Impact
Supports demand planning decisions and helps optimize inventory allocation to reduce holding costs.
